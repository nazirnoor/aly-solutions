Frequently asked

Questions before booking

Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a note.

Where are sessions held?

All private sessions are held at our Ampang studio in Dataran Palma, inside Grand Barakah Hotel. Corporate workshops can be hosted at your office in the Klang Valley.

Do you specialise in modest wear?

Yes — modest styling and hijab coordination is a core part of our practice, and we work with all dress codes from corporate to bridal.

How long until I see results?

Most clients leave the first session with an immediate shift in confidence and a clear blueprint they can act on the same week.

Do you shop on my behalf?

Yes. Personal shopping is offered as an add-on and can be arranged in KL malls or curated online.
